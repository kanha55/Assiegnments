1.A person crosses a 600 m long street in 5 minutes. What is his speed in km per hour?

A.	3.6
B.	7.2
C.	8.4
D.	10 
Answer:-
Speed =		600	m/sec.
5 x 60
   = 2 m/sec.

Converting m/sec to km/hr (see important formulas section)

   =		2 x	18		km/hr
5
   = 7.2 km/hr.
